Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my repair take?

+

Repair times vary depending on the extent of damage. After a thorough inspection, we'll provide you with an accurate timeline. Most minor repairs take 3-5 days, while major collision work can take 1-2 weeks.

Do you offer a warranty on your repairs?

+

Yes! We offer a lifetime warranty on all our repairs for as long as you own your vehicle. This covers both workmanship and materials.

Will my insurance cover the repairs?

+

We work with all major insurance companies. Our team will help you navigate the claims process and communicate directly with your adjuster to ensure proper coverage.

Do I need an appointment for an estimate?

+

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize your wait time. We also offer virtual estimates via photos for your convenience.
